About me

Designer who actually ships.

I'm Paige — a 24-year-old interface designer from New Zealand with a Bachelor of Design (Interface Design) from the University of Waikato and a habit of making things look significantly better than I found them.

Most recently I was a Marketing Coordinator at a luxury lighting company, where I rebuilt their website from scratch, created print collateral, ran social media, and made sure everything from spec sheets to in-building signage had that polished feeling. Before that: a design internship, and three years at NZ Post where I snuck in design work wherever I could.

I work at the intersection of visual design and front-end development — equally at home in Figma or a code editor, equally happy with a rebrand or a responsive layout. Every graphic, illustration, and image across my work is hand-drawn by me in Illustrator.
