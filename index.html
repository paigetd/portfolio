About me

Designer who actually ships.

I'm Paige — a 24-year-old interface designer from New Zealand with a Bachelor of Design (Interface Design) from the University of Waikato and a habit of making things look significantly better than I found them.

Most recently I was a Marketing Coordinator at a luxury lighting company, where I rebuilt their website from the ground up, created print collateral, ran social media, and made sure everything from the spec sheets to the signage had that polished feeling. Before that: a design internship, and three years at NZ Post where I snuck in design work wherever I could.

I work at the intersection of visual design and front-end development — comfortable in both Figma and a code editor, equally at home with a rebrand or a responsive layout.
